Voldemort Scientist

Voldemort a.k.a. Tom Marvolo Riddle, is referred to as the evilest wizard in the Harry Potter wizarding world. His goal in life is to live forever and be all-powerful. Voldemort killed Harry Potter's parents and became his main enemy.

  1. Baldness: Voldemort is known for his complete lack of hair, including eyebrows and eyelashes.
  2. Red Eyes: His eyes are described as having a dark red or scarlet color, which adds to his unsettling appearance.
  3. Pale Skin: Voldemort's skin is exceptionally pale, almost white, which contrasts sharply with his dark robes.
